My name is Xiaoli Su. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Transportation Engineering from Fujian University of Technology and a Master’s degree in Management from Fuzhou University. I am currently pursuing a joint PhD program in Management at emlyon Business School and Pôle Universitaire Léonard de Vinci (ESILV).

我的名字是苏晓丽。我拥有福建理工大学的交通运输学学士学位和 福州大学的管理学硕士学位。目前，我正在里昂商学院（emlyon）和 福州大学联合培养的管理学博士项目中攻读学位。

I enjoy traveling and photography because exploring new places and capturing beautiful moments allow me to appreciate the world’s diversity and creativity. Each trip inspires me, and every photograph tells a unique story.

我热爱旅行和摄影，因为探索新地方和捕捉美好瞬间让我能够欣赏世界的多样性与创造力。每一次旅行都带给我灵感，每一张照片都讲述着独特的故事。
